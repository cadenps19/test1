Previous Job Experience

City of Springfield Environmental Services — CONTRACT WORKER

At my current job with the City of Springfield working in Environmental Services I repair and maintain sewer mains throughout the city. My responsibilities are self-initiating. When I get to my job I start out by cleaning out our truck and preparing our tools and supplies for the day's task. Then I proceed to fuel up the work truck, skid steer, track hoe, backhoe, and the excavator. I then load the machines onto their respective trailers and connect the trailers to the tandem dump trucks. Then I gather the required materials for the build, including: fernco couplings, lateral hookups, glue and primer, 8 inch and 4 inch PVC pipe required for the sewer repair. I make sure to talk to the home-owners yard we will be working in to make certain that they will be happy with the work we will be doing so as to not cause an inconvenience. I then make sure to Keep a safe working environment while the operator uses various machines as stated previously to dig a safe hole to work in. Once the hole is dug by the operator and I have followed and maintained the safety protocols, such as shoring the hole properly, cleaning up the area around the hole to ensure nothing falls in when not intended, and wearing personal protective equipment. I proceed to go down the hole and clean around the broken pipe so I can then replace it with the new pipe to keep everyone's sewer working to the expected degree. Once the job is completed it is important to me to go to the home owner and make sure that they are satisfied with the work. I finish off my day by cleaning the tools we used and getting the machines ready for the next day's work.
